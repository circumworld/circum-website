  • Whitelist

    All Token Purchases will be done by registered whitelisted participants and must go through a KYC / AML process.

  • How can I request to join the Whitelist?

    Joining our whitelist, plus staying up to date with circum.world is easy. On our web site, simply provide your contact email and subscribe. Our community manager will reach out to you and complete the whitelist processing, as well as answer any further questions you may have about the Token Sales Event or the circum.world commerce community.

  • Will unsold tokens be burned?

    Yes.

  • How will the funds received be used?

    The funds raised within the Token Sales Event will support the continued development and launching of the project in a service platform model. The circum.world commerce community will initially deploy in emerging markets, where millions/billions of people have no or little access to traditional banking and payments. Funds will be used across various part of the business including Technology Development, Security, User Acquisition & Marketing, Legal, Admin, Community Incentives and Operations.

  • How can I get involved in Circum.world?

    At circum.world we are a community. Together we are building the dominant design for the next generation of financial plumbing to support the needs of billions of people around the world. We believe in financial equality, which will support not only the individual, but also the collective. We are leveraging the simple economic concepts that the more efficiently, frequently and effectively any two parties transact, so shall they both individually and collectively grow. circum.world is a fresh and unique new approach to the world of commerce. Open for business and this time everyone is welcome! If you’d like to help or contribute to the project, please drop us a line at hello@circum.world or join the conversation on telegram https://t.me/circumworld.
